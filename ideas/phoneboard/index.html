Your Mobile Phone is a Skateboard

Did you ever found yourself throwing your phone in the air and trying to do some tricks you remember from the times you where out there with your skateboard? ( by out there I ‘m of course talking about the streets of Tony Hawks Pro Skater )

Why don’t you try to use the capabilities of your phone to recognise the movements of your phone and build an awesome game with that?

This would be an exciting game. You really have to have balls to throw your 700€ IPhone into the air!
